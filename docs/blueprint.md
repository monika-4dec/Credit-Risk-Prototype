# **App Name**: CreditWise Sentinel

## Core Features:

- Portfolio Risk Overview: Provides a high-level view of the borrower portfolio, including risk distribution and trends, with interactive charts.
- Borrower Profile Analysis: Presents a consolidated view of borrower data, including loan details, traditional credit scores, and AI-driven cognitive risk scores.
- AI-Powered Risk Scoring: Leverages AI to analyze customer-banker conversations and assess borrower risk, providing an overall risk classification.
- Conversation Analysis Timeline: Displays a timeline of customer-banker interactions, with conversation transcripts and highlighted behavioral cues.
- Behavioral Insights Panel: Visually explains AI signals, including sentiment trends, detected intents, and conversation dynamics.
- Reason Code Explanations: Explains why a borrower was flagged, highlighting top behavioral drivers and linguistic signals, acting as a tool to provide banker-friendly explanations.
- Actionable Recommendations Engine: Recommends next steps based on AI insights, such as initiating check-in calls or considering early renegotiation.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep Blue (#1A5276) to inspire trust and confidence.
- Background color: Light Grey (#E5E8E8) for a clean and professional banking UI.
- Accent color: Soft Lavender (#AEB6FF) for interactive elements and highlighting key insights.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter' (sans-serif) for a modern and objective feel; suitable for both headlines and body text.
- Use a set of consistent, professional icons (outline style) to represent different risk factors and actions.
- Employ a modular card-based layout for clear organization of information. Responsive design for desktop-first approach.
- Subtle transition animations to smoothly reveal additional details or insights on user interaction.
